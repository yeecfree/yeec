YouTuber Estimated Earning Calculator

YEEC (YouTuber Estimated Earning Calculator) can only calculate the estimated Google Adsense revenue based on total views and cost per thousand views!

Keep In Mind :: Only Ad displayed video generates Ad revenue

Equation ::

Ev = Effective Views
Tv = Total Video Views
Sv = Subtract No Ad Views

Rmin = Minimum Revenue
Rmax = Maximum Revenue

CPTmin = Minimum Cost Per Thousand Views
CPTmax = Maximum Cost Per Thousand Views
CPT also called as CPM (Cost Per Milli)

1 Thousand (K) =1000
1 Lakh (L) = 100 Thousand
1 Million (M) = 10 Lakh
1 Billion (B) = 1000 Million
1 Trillion (T) = 1000 Billion

A good YouTuber revenue can be generated in several ways ::
1. Display Advertisements
2. Crowd Funding / Fan Funding
3. Sponsorship / Influencer
4. Merchandise
5. Affiliate Marketing
